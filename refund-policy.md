# AXARA Bridge — Refund Policy

**Last updated: April 2025**

---

## 1. Beta Period

Tier 1 access is currently free during the beta period. 
No payment is required and no refund policy applies to 
beta access.

---

## 2. Our Commitment

When paid tiers launch, AXARA BioScience stands behind 
the quality of every analytical report. If you are not 
satisfied we will make it right.

---

## 3. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

- The report failed to generate due to a technical error 
  on our side
- You were charged more than once for the same submission
- The service was unavailable at the time of your 
  submission and you were charged
- You contact us within 7 days of purchase

---

## 4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are not available if:

- A complete report was successfully delivered
- The uploaded file was not in centroided .mzML format 
  and no results were found as a direct consequence
- The experiment did not produce SDA labelling and 
  therefore no SDA hits were identified — the search 
  engine performed correctly on the data provided
- The request is made more than 7 days after purchase
- You disagree with the scientific interpretation of 
  results — the report is a computational analytical 
  output not a guarantee of experimental outcome

---

## 5. File Format Responsibility

AXARA Bridge requires centroided .mzML input files. 
It is the responsibility of the user to ensure files 
are correctly converted before upload. Refunds are 
not provided for empty results caused by profile-mode 
or incorrectly formatted input files.

We recommend verifying your file format using 
ProteoWizard msconvert before upload.

---

## 6. How to Request a Refund

Email refunds@axara.bio within 7 days of purchase with:

- Your order number from Paddle
- The filename submitted
- A brief description of the issue

We will respond within 2 business days and process 
approved refunds within 5-7 business days via Paddle.
