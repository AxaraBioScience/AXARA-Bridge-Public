# AXARA Bridge — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2025**

---

## 1. Zero Retention Policy

AXARA BioScience operates a strict Zero Retention Policy.

All .mzML files uploaded to AXARA Bridge are processed 
exclusively in volatile RAM. No uploaded files, peptide 
sequences, protein identifications, or report content 
are stored, logged, retained, or shared with any third 
party after report generation is complete.

When your browser session ends your data is gone.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate 
the service and improve it during beta:

**Beta registration data:**
- Full name
- Institution name
- Work email address
- Professional role

This data is collected at beta registration and used 
solely for:
- Communicating service updates and feedback requests
- Notifying you when paid tiers launch
- Understanding who is using the service to improve it

**Anonymous usage data:**
- Number of files processed (no file content)
- Processing time
- General geographic region

No personally identifiable information is linked to 
usage data.

---

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect

- .mzML file contents
- Peptide sequences or protein identifications
- Report content or download history
- IP addresses linked to analytical submissions
- Any molecular or chemical structure data

---

## 4. Payment Processing

Tier 1 is free during beta. When paid tiers launch, 
payment processing will be handled by Paddle.com who 
act as Merchant of Record. AXARA BioScience will never 
see or store payment card details. Paddle's privacy 
policy applies to all payment transactions.

---

## 5. Cookies

The service uses session cookies only to maintain your 
beta access state within a single browser session. 
These cookies are not persistent and are deleted when 
the browser session ends.

No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or 
third-party analytics cookies are used.

---

## 6. Third Party Services

The service runs on Google Cloud Run infrastructure. 
Google's infrastructure privacy policy applies to 
server-level operations. No analytical data is passed 
to Google or any other third party.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law you have the right 
to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. 
The only personal data we may hold is your beta 
registration information. Contact us at any time to 
request access, correction, or deletion.

Given our Zero Retention Policy, no analytical data 
can be retrieved or deleted because none is stored.

---

## 8. Data Security

Beta registration data is stored securely with access 
limited to AXARA BioScience personnel. Analytical data 
is never stored — it cannot be breached because it 
does not persist.

---

## 9. Children

AXARA Bridge is a professional scientific tool intended 
for use by qualified researchers. It is not directed 
at or intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age.

---

## 10. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. We will notify 
beta users of material changes by email. Continued use 
of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated 
policy.
